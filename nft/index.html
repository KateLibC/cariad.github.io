NFT

No.

The only things NFTs have given us are shitty monkey images, hexagons on Twitter, and an ever-increasing amount of carbon dioxide emitted into our atmosphere.

You cannot be for fighting climate change all the while churning away computer processing power on something which has no physical analogue or genuine purpose other than showing off wealth. Your NFT is in an essence a goddamn hyperlink and nothing more.

They're just modern-day Pogs--at least with them they can be recycled or melted down into something useful. Even the creator of NFTs, Anil Dash has disowned them.

If you want to support artists, commission them or don't buy knock-off products.
